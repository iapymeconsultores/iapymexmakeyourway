Design Thinking Layer

Structured problem-solving framework developed over three years across four countries. Buyer persona templates, COCD Box ideation, rapid prototyping methods, and validation playbooks. Guided step-by-step with proprietary materials at every stage.

AI Acceleration Layer

At each stage, AI tools amplify speed and output quality. Research with Perplexity, prototype with v0 and Cursor, validate with AI-generated landing pages, prepare pitches with AI-assisted storytelling. Technology as multiplier, not replacement.
